## 1. The World Map: Soundia
Soundia was once a land of crystal-clear melodies and perfect rhymes until the **Static Scrambler** arrived.

### The Biomes (Environment Concepts)
1.  **Beatbox Basin (Brio's Turf):** 
    - *Vibe:* Neon-Jungle / 80s Retro-Futurism.
    - *Visuals:* Glowing mushrooms that pulse like speakers, teal vines shaped like audio cables.
2.  **Consonant Quarry (Diesel's Turf):** 
    - *Vibe:* Industrial-Playground / Construction Chic.
    - *Visuals:* Floating girders made of pure 'B' and 'D' sounds, yellow dust that sparkles with stardust.
3.  **Vowel Valley (Vowelia's Turf):** 
    - *Vibe:* Ethereal Loom / Dreamscape.
    - *Visuals:* Giant luminous spider-webs weaving 'A-E-I-O-U' into fabric, purple aurora borealis overhead.
4.  **Speedway Springs (Zippy's Turf):** 
    - *Vibe:* High-Octane Arcade / Synthwave.
    - *Visuals:* Red plasma tracks, clouds that look like exhaust smoke from a jet engine.

## 2. Finalized Character Model Sheets

### **Antagonist: The Static Scrambler**
- **Form:** Amorphous cloud of gray noise. 
- **Key Details:** Jagged lightning eyebrows, red-glitch eyes, trailing "broken" pixels.
- **Personality:** Existential dread of clarity.

### **Brio the Beatboxer**
- **Form:** Bipedal Arctic Fox.
- **Key Details:** Oversized teal headphones (Master V3), tech-vest with volume sliders, constant "head-bob" idle animation.
- **Voice Profile (Gemini 'Kore'):** Deep, rhythmic, warm, "Everything is a beat."

### **Diesel the Digger**
- **Form:** Mechanical Alligator/Turtle Hybrid.
- **Key Details:** Yellow hard hat with a diamond-drill horn, hydraulic legs, tail shaped like a backhoe.
- **Voice Profile (Gemini 'Kore'):** Deep, gravelly, encouraging, "Let's dig into these sounds."

### **Vowelia**
- **Form:** Six-armed Silk-Moth Queen.
- **Key Details:** Translucent purple wings, crown of glowing threads, each hand holding a different "vowel needle."
- **Voice Profile (Gemini 'Puck'):** Soft, airy, magical, wise, "Weave the music of the words."

### **Zippy the Zoomer**
- **Form:** Cybernetic Hummingbird.
- **Key Details:** Twin red jet-turbines on back, goggles with HUD display, streaks of red light trailing every movement.
- **Voice Profile (Gemini 'Zephyr'):** High-energy, rapid-fire, enthusiastic, "Fastest sounds in the west!"

## 3. Audio Style Guide
- **Musical Genre:** "Educational Synth-Pop" ‚Äî catchy, low-latency loops that avoid cognitive overload.
- **SFX Philosophy:** 
  - *Correct:* Harmonic 'Ding' with a character-specific layer (Beatbox snare for Brio, Magic harp for Vowelia).
  - *Incorrect:* Soft "Static puff" (The Scrambler's signature sound). Never harsh or punishing.
- **Voice Pacing:** 
  - Standard: 1.0x.
  - Zippy Default: 1.25x (Energetic).
  - Adaptive Scaffolding: 0.8x (Triggered after 2 errors).
